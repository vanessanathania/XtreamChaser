GAMEPLAY

1.Main menu

Welcome to the game, you can choose mode what you want to play. If you are new player, we suggest to practice first.

2.Fight

After you choose your character, you must use your skills to defeat the enemy. Find combos to quickly conquer enemies.

3.Procedure Fight

There are 3 stages in which there are 2 rounds each. If one stage wins or loses it will be determined at the final stage who will win.

4.Result

After that there is a result and when the result wins, there will be a level up, from the lowest to the highest level such as Beginner, Master, GrandMaster, Emperor, Expert and Initiate. If you lose then there will be no leveling up

5.Upgrade Characters

Don't forget to always ugrade and customize your characters to get infinity wins.
