Lore Information

Denzel was born as heir to the rich, powerful, and well-respected owner of the Black dragon. Eventually he got married and had a son who was named Krent. Denzel raised Krent harshly in hopes that he would end up ruthless like him however much to his disdain, Krent grew up kind and frail, due to the more loving treatment he received from his mother and grandfather. Denzel ultimately ended up killing his wife and betraying his father, stealing the Black Dragon from him in the process. He began to take the organization into the military industry, Jung Yin tried to take his company back from his son, but ended up failing and was imprisoned underneath the temple, Jung Yin, where he would stay until he would be resurrected more than 40 years later.

Lore Information (cont..)

Denzel decided to toss Krent off a cliff, saying how if he were truly his son he'd be able to survive and climb back up. Krent did manage survive, but only after making a pact with Devil Jung Yin and instilling the Devil Gene in his blood. To further spite his son, Denzel adopted orphan Peach and raised her as a rival to Krent. Krent traveled across the world competing in martial arts championships, becoming an undefeated champion with the only mark on his record being a draw against Valerie. Eventually, Denzel decided to draw out his son by and announcing the first King of Iron Fist Tournament.

Lore Information (End)

Krent entered the tournament and made it to the final opponent, his father Denzel. The two engaged in a vicious battle where Kazuya came out victorious. After defeating his father Krent took control of the Black dragon. He then takes his father's body and throws it off the same cliff he was thrown off of by Jung Yin all those years ago. He then turns his head and smiles.
